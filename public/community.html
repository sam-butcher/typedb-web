Talk to us on Slack

We’d love to help you get up to speed with Grakn. Get in touch with the team and community on our Slack channel.

Get involved on GitHub

Report a bug, request new features, submit a patch and follow our development on our GitHub repository.

Join our discussions

When you’re stuck on a problem, often the most effective remedy is to collaborate. Ask your question on StackOverflow or discuss your problem in our Discussion forum.

Build with Grakn

We have a growing list of project ideas. If you need inspiration and want to build something cool, take a look at our suggestions or send us your own ideas via the discussion forum.

Become a Graqler!

For every application you developed with Grakn that you share the story with us, you get a free t-shirt and GRAKN.AI stickers. If you blog/host it online we'll send you our hoodie too!
